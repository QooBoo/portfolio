We have finaly figured out how we want to distribute our portfolio, it will be a game:
1
Mint or buy limited NFT (2500 NFTportfolio)
2
All the holders of our NFT – are mining NFTp directly on the site (internal currency). Nothing to set up, just connect your wallet. Each NFT will have NP Boost from 10 to 40. This is actual daily mining speed. NFT Boost 17 will simply bring you 17 NFTp a day.

FAQ's
When, how much, how many?

The total supply is 2500 NFT (ERC-721), priced at 0,12 ETH. 500 whitelists and 5 mints per wallet. Mint is coming of 5th, May

What is NFTportfolio? What is their value?

This is limited number of NFT. Using them you will be able to mint unlimited number of keys for portfolio users.

How can I get NFTp?

All owners of our NFTportfolio are mining on site (using internal currency). For mining you just need to connect your wallet. Each NFT will have NFT Boost from 10 to 40. This is actual mining speed per day. If your NFT NP Boost is 17, it means that you will get 17 a day.

How can I get my NFTkey?

NFTkey unlimited amount of NFT EPC-721, using them you will be able to start using portfolio. NFTkey could be minted for NFTp. Consider that after minting a block of 2500 NFTkeys, the cost of the next 2500 will be 25NFTp increase. Starting price is 100 NFTp, this was done in order to make them harder to get which will positevly impact their own growth and NFTportfolio.
